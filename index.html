About Pi Kappa Phi - Delta Psi

Welcome to the Delta Psi Chapter of Pi Kappa Phi Fraternity where our members take pride in being Men Of Class. Located at the University of Texas at Arlington, we are a group of talented, dedicated and motivated men who have the desire to give back and take lead. Along with owning and running our own Philanthropy, we are proud to say we raise over $5000 each year for PUSH America which caters to those with disabilities. We are also glad to say that we have the Most Improved Chapter GPA in Greek life among other various awards and recognitions. We are proud to be members of Pi Kappa Phi and we hope you enjoy learning about us as much as we do. Pi Kapps Are We!
